The Team

Analytical.
Attentive.
In your corner.

Four coaches. Four styles. One shared obsession: getting you better, faster, without breaking you in the process. Every session you'll train with at least one of them.

Head Coach · Founder

Andrew

Architect of the Flow Method. Andrew draws from biomechanics, strategy, and theoretical physics to build curricula that actually compound. Relentlessly analytical. Surprisingly patient.

Senior Coach · Technique

Adam

Our technical specialist. If you want to know why your cross keeps landing short, Adam will tell you, precisely, kinematically, and in plain English. Works best with trainees who like detail.

Lead Coach · Women's Program

Jess

Leads our weekday Female Mornings. Builds confidence quickly in first-timers, pushes experienced fighters harder than they expect. Co-founder of the gym with Andrew. The heart of the room.

Coach · Juniors & Movement

Toby

Runs the kids program and handles movement coaching across the gym. Believes boxing is footwork with a punch on top. Patient with beginners, sharper than he looks in the ring.

Coaching Philosophy

We coach people,
not programs.

A good coach doesn't run you through their curriculum, they build one around you. That's why every class at Flow Sparring includes personalised, one-on-one feedback, even when the room is full.

We watch. We note. We adjust. You leave each session with one specific thing to work on, not a highlight reel of mistakes. Skill is built one correction at a time.
