Programs

Four programs.
One method.

Whether it's your first time lacing gloves or you've been in the ring for a decade, there's a program here for you. All classes use the Flow Method and all include live, damage-free sparring.

Program 01 · Drop-In

Open Mat
Boxing.

Ten hours a day, five days a week. Walk in whenever it suits your schedule and a coach will put you through the work. Skip the rigid class format, this is boxing training that flexes around your life.

  • A
    Who it's forAnyone, total beginner through competition-ready.
  • B
    What you'll learnStance, footwork, combinations, defence, timing, built up progressively.
  • C
    WhenMonday through Friday, 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM.

Program 02 · Women Only

Female
Mornings.

Three morning time slots: 6:15 AM, 7:45 AM, and 9:15 AM, every weekday. A female-only training environment led by our coach Jess. No pretence, no ego, no judgement. Just boxing, technique, and community.

  • A
    Who it's forWomen of all ages and abilities, beginners welcome and prioritised.
  • B
    What you'll learnFull boxing curriculum with a specific focus on body mechanics and leverage.
  • C
    WhenWeekdays at 6:15 AM, 7:45 AM, and 9:15 AM.
Book your first session
Program 03 · Weekends

Saturday
Sessions.

Our busiest, best-energy day of the week. Eight hours of mixed open mat, families, couples, solo trainers, visiting fighters. Bring a friend. Grab a coffee at Norton Plaza after. Best introduction to the gym there is.

  • A
    Who it's forEveryone, including first-timers and anyone bringing a guest.
  • B
    What you'll learnTechnique and sparring mixed in. Flexible format, coach-guided.
  • C
    WhenSaturdays, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM.
Book your first session
Program 04 · Juniors

Kids
Classes.

Age-appropriate boxing for children, built around confidence, focus, fitness, and coordination. Not aggression. Every class is supervised, structured, and fun. After-school slots and Saturday mornings.

  • A
    Who it's forChildren ages 6–14. Groups split by age and level.
  • B
    What they'll learnMovement, technique, respect for the sport, focus, and self-control.
  • C
    WhenWeekdays 3:30 PM & 4:00 PM. Saturdays 9:00 AM & 9:30 AM.
